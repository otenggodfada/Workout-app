Calf Raises


Calf Raises

Body part:
calf

Description:
1) Lift your heels off the floor while keeping your toes on the floor and your knees straight. You should raise your heels high enough that you feel a strong tightining of the muscles in the back of your leg(calf muscles) without pain or cramping. 2) hold up for a count of 5 then lower slowly.
